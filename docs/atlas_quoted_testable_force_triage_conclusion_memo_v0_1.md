## Triage bar

A price-only force clears for wake-design consideration only if it has all four:

1. credible expected forecastable content beyond its cluster baseline;
2. adequate testable sample density / power;
3. acceptable crowdedness / already-priced prior;
4. governance cost justified versus the free knowledge-mode alternatives.

Knowledge-mode alternatives, such as writing or specifying cards, spend no sealed attempt.

A sealed wake attempt would spend one of the shared attempts under the Atlas governance budget.

Explicit guards:

- Observable before move is not sufficient for edge.
- Observable before move is a descriptive column, not a verdict.
- Interesting is not a ranking column.
- Interest is at most a post-evidence tiebreaker, never a primary axis.
- No candidate is claimed to have edge anywhere in this memo.
